## Caption

SigDigger is an open-source digital signal analyzer.

Ships with:
- NOAA weather satellite images in real time
- Aircraft transponder decoding
- Pager, walkie-talkie & analog voice interception
- Live waterfall + full spectrum analysis
- Custom plugins & SoapySDR support for almost every SDR
- Built from scratch (Suscan + Sigutils)

No license. No subscription. Just plug and dig.

> 👉 https://github.com/BatchDrake/SigDigger
