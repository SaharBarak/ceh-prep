Just go to claude.ai and start prompting.

Here are 7 prompts every cybersecurity engineer should save:
Sigma Rule Generation: Sigma is a generic detection rule format that works across any SIEM. Describe the attack, get a deployable rule.

YARA Rule Writing: YARA is a pattern-matching tool for identifying malware. Define your indicators, get a scanning rule.
CIS Hardening Guide: CIS Benchmarks are the industry-standard security baselines for OS and cloud hardening. Pick your OS, get exact commands.

K8s NetworkPolicy: NetworkPolicies control pod-to-pod traffic in Kubernetes clusters. Describe the segmentation, get a zero-trust YAML.

Adversary Simulation Plan: Adversary simulation replicates real threat actor TTPs mapped to MITRE ATT&CK. Pick a threat group, get a full exercise plan.
